Key Insights & Discussion

  • Random Forest outperforms due to its ability to capture non-linear interactions and reduce noise variance.
  • Linear models underperform (R² ≈ 0) confirming weak linear structure in financial markets.
  • Sentiment signals show predictive relevance but require domain-specific NLP (limitation of TextBlob).
  • Post-leakage correction accuracy (~60%) aligns with realistic financial modeling benchmarks.

Conclusion & Future Scope

This system demonstrates a scalable framework for integrating unstructured news with structured financial data for predictive analytics.
